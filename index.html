About TAY Forge Initiative

TAY Forge Initiative is a community-based organization dedicated to rescuing, mentoring, and empowering street children, teenagers, and youth. We provide shelter, education, mentorship, and life skills training to help vulnerable children transition from street life to safe, stable and productive futures.

Our work focuses on forging resilience, purpose and hope — guiding young people from hardship to empowerment and equipping them to become active contributors to their communities.

Our Focus Pillars

Rescue & Support

Immediate care: shelter, food, healthcare, and reintegration support. Education and counseling to restore hope and rebuild lives.

Mentorship & Empowerment

Mentorship programs for teenagers and youth, leadership training, life skills, and guidance for education and careers.

Community Development

Youth-led community projects, vocational training, entrepreneurship and technology workshops that build local capacity and create opportunities.

Programs & Activities

  • Street Outreach: Mobile teams engage with children on the streets to offer immediate support and pathways to shelter.
  • Shelter & Reintegration: Short-term safe housing, tutoring, and family reunification or vocational placement.
  • Workshops: Technology basics, entrepreneurship, arts and trades for youth development.
  • Mental Health & Counseling: Trauma-informed care, peer support groups and individual counseling.
